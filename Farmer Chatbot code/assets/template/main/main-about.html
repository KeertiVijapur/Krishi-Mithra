About Us

Krishi Mitra is a smart agricultural assistant designed to empower farmers with instant advice, crop guidance, and sustainable farming solutions through AI-powered technology.
We’re Krishi Mitra

Krishi Mitra aims to bridge the gap between farmers and technology by providing easy-to-access information about crops, fertilizers, weather updates, and pest management. Our chatbot interacts in simple language to help farmers make informed decisions.

With Krishi Mitra, farmers can receive personalized recommendations, government scheme details, and market insights – all in one place. Our mission is to make farming smarter, more efficient, and sustainable for every grower.

Our Office

Our headquarters are located in ISE-BLDEACET, Karnataka – the hub of agricultural innovation and technology. From here, our team works to deliver reliable AI-based farming assistance to users across India.

We collaborate with agricultural experts, government departments, and research institutes to ensure farmers receive the most accurate and region-specific information.

Krishi Mitra also operates through a network of local partners to reach rural areas and empower farmers with digital literacy. Our chatbot is available 24/7 to answer questions, making farming support accessible anytime, anywhere.
